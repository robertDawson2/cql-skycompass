Fitness: p.r.e.p. Program

p.r.e.p®. is The Arena Club's Pysician Referred Exercise Program. Through your physican's referral you can join The Arena Club for 60 days for $60. p.r.e.p. participants meet with The Arena Club medical fitness professionals twice per week for 60 days, in small group settings.

frequently asked p.r.e.p.® questions

How much will p.r.e.p cost?
The p.r.e.p program costs only $1 per day, totaling $60 for 60 days.

How much help will I receive?
You will work with a medical fitness specialist two times per week in small group sessions.

Will I be able to use The Arena Club outside of my twice weekly appointments?
Yes, you will have a fitness membership to The Arena Club during the 60 days. Come enjoy the indoor amenities at The Arena Club as frequently as you like!

I do not have much time to exercise. How long will my session last?
We understand you have a busy schedule, so to ensure success, we only ask you to complete two 30-minute workout sessions each week.

What else do I receive?
Each p.r.e.p participant receives:

• Group nutrition session
• 15% discount on any additional wellness programs
• Introduction to Personal Training & Aquatic Exercise
• Access to all group exercise classes

How do I get started?
Ask your physician if you are a candidate for the program. If so, your physician simply completes the referral form faxes it to The Arena Club.

Your medical fitness professional will contact you within 48 hours to get you on track to achieving a healthier, more active lifestyle. Now is the time!

What happens after p.r.e.p?
The Arena Club hopes p.r.e.p “graduates” will embrace what they have learned, and continue on track with a new, healthier lifestyle. We have many incentives for p.r.e.p grads if they decide to stay at The Arena Club (to find out more, speak with any of our p.r.e.p. team members).

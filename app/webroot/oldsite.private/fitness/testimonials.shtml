Lori's Story

I am a 46 year old female standing at 5'9''. I was generally a small build, but having my 3rd child at age 37 and my 4th child at age 41, my metabolism changed and I started packing the weight on. In Spring 2011, I weighed in at 242 pounds, was
diagnosed with diabetes, high blood pressure and high
cholesterol and put on medication for all. I was placing
everyone else's needs before my own.

Through the summer months I dropped about 25 pounds
dieting alone. In October 2011, I weighed in at 215 pounds.
I began coming to The Arena Club in November, using the
elliptical for as long as I could 3 days a week, because that was what I was familiar with. The pounds started melting away as my endurance and muscle tone began to increase.

It is now May 2012 (1 year later) and my weight has dropped to 158. I no longer need any meds for any illnesses, I am no
longer required to test blood sugars, and I was told by the
doctor that I could eat as much fruit as I want. I changed my diet to high fiber whole grains and non-processed foods. I am currently still working the elliptical, at this time, on level 10 for 1 hour, 3-4 times a week, frontwards and backwards, usually
covering at least 7 miles per workout.

Since late October, I have dropped 57 pounds that I attribute to exercise, as diet alone could not have accomplished this. I was told by my nutritionist to eat a high fiber, no sugar, and low salt diet. With this advice and exercise I have been able to drop 84 pounds in the last year. I still have 8 pounds to go and surgery to have hernias removed and abdominal muscles reattached (they were ripped in my last pregnancy). But, all in all, I feel very confident about what I have
accomplished- putting my health as first priority this last year. I am a better Mom for it!

Frank Mezzanotte
All-American defenseman at Towson University; Two-time member of All-South Atlantic Lacrosse Team; Former Head Coach at Edgewood High School (State Champions 1983, 1984 & 1989); Harford County Coach of the Year for 1982, 1984, 1985 and 1989; All-Metro Coach of the Year for 1983; Former Head Coach of the Baltimore Tribe professional lacrosse team; 1990 Leukemia All-Metro Team Coach; Coach of 1989 All-South High School All Star Team; Towson University Athletic Hall of Fame; Former Head Coach at Harford Community College; 1994 College Coach of the Year.

Coach Mezzanotte has been involved with lacrosse since 1977 when the Board of Education in Harford County, Maryland approved the inclusion of lacrosse into the interscholastic sports venue. In his 35 plus years of coaching experience which included recreation, high school, college and the professional ranks he has always brought his enthusiasm and knowledge at every level. He has also co-founded Harford Lacrosse Camp which has been running strong 30 plus years for boys and girls ages 5-18. for Coach Mezzanotte is also proud to boost of his teaching career which spans 35 plus years. He is a certified k-12 teacher and has taught at every level with elementary being his favorite age of kids. Coach Mezzanotte lacrosse accolades includes All-American defenseman at Towson University; Two-time member of All-South Atlantic Lacrosse Team; Former Head Coach at Edgewood High School (State Champions 1983, 1984 & 1989); Harford County Coach of the Year for 1982, 1984, 1985 and 1989; All-Metro Coach of the Year for 1983; Former Head Coach of the Baltimore Tribe professional lacrosse team; 1990 Leukemia All-Metro Team Coach; Coach of 1989 All-South High School All Star Team; Towson University Athletic Hall of Fame; Former Head Coach at Harford Community College; 1994 College Coach of the Year.

His philosophy is that “Every Kids counts”. His likes to worked with each kid individually to and improve their weaknesses and build confidence through their strengths. The Lax Starz Academy is a great program for players of all abilities. Coach Mezzanotte focuses on repetition of fundamentals, which are needed to have success at higher levels of lacrosse. Most college coaches are not impressed with the “razzle, dazzle stuff” but want kids to come into a college setting with the proper fundamentals to succeed. The Lax Starz Academy has also an onsite sport performance trainer during their strength and conditioning. We used the state of the art technology through sports cords, dynamic warm-ups, reaction drills, etc. to prepare your player for their upcoming season.

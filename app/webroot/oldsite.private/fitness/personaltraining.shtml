Certified Personal Trainers will provide structure and accountability for your workouts and show you how to develop habits that promote and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

If you are new to exercise, a Personal Trainer can help you become familiar with our equipment and give you knowledge and confidence to develop your fitness program.

Health issues? Your trainer can work with you and your health care provider to plan a safe program that considers your needs.

Have you been working out regularly but have hit a plateau? A trainer can help you change up your routine and move poast plateaus to help you achieve your goals. No matter what fitness level or goal you have, a personal trainer can help you get there!

Personal trainers at the Arena Club are held to the highest standards. All our trainers are required to be certified through a nationally accredited personal training program and are required to fulfill annual continuing education standards.

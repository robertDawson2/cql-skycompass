Kids Programs: Pre-K Enrichment Program

The Arena Club Enrichment Program provides a quality preschool program for children ages 3-5 years old.  Our goal is to provide your child with daily opportunities to enrich their academic and social skills through creative learning, educational experiences and physical development. Enrolling your child in our program will also allow your child to have access to our indoor field and swimming pool during class time!  Classes are held Monday through Friday, September thru June, our preschool welcomes members and non-members to attend.

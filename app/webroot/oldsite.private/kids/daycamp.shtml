Kids Programs: Day Camp

Don't let your child stay home with nothing to do on a day off from school. Let them come and play with us! Your child will enjoy a fun-filled day of indoor arena sports, outdoor games and activities, swimming year round, crafts and much more!  Camp runs when Harford County Schools are closed from 8am-6pm and is available to children ages Kindergarten - 8th Grade.

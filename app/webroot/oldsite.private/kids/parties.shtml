Kids Programs: Parties

Celebrate your birthday at the Arena Club where we bring a new meaning to fun!

Parents: Let Us Do It All!

Parents, kids and teens all over Harford County have discovered the unique experience of The Arena Club birthday party! Our party and event staff is renowned for giving every party the personal touch! You and your group will enjoy a unique and fun experience making your special day all the more memorable!

Basic Birthday Party package includes:

  • Up to 18 children as guests (birthday child not included in that number - birthday child is free)
    • $15 additional fee for every child over the 18 guest limit
    • Maximum number of 24 total children
  • 18 party invitations
  • 2 party hosts to serve your party
  • 3 large cheese pizzas and 4 2-liter bottles of Pepsi beverages
  • All paper products & utensils (plates, napkins, cups, forks, spoons, and tablecloths)
  • Balloons for party area

Deluxe Package includes ($50 additional charge to basic package):

  • Everything in Basic Package plus
  • Ice Cream per child
  • Party favors per child

Member $349 , Non-Member $399

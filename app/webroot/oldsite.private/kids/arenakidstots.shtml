Kids Programs: Tots & Kids

Click here for the Arena Tots & Kids Schedule!

Arena Tots & Kids is designed to offer children ages 2 1/2-11 years old structured play using our indoor field, swimming pools, outside playground, basketball & volleyball courts, and much more! These classes are scheduled during the peak workout hours for moms and dads.  Parents will know that while they are getting a great workout their child will have lots of fun all while being active and socializing with friends...best of all, these classes are included in your family membership! 

All activities will take place in designated locations throughout the club, please   refer to the schedule on the back.  Classes are broken up into two different age groups, Arena Tots is for children ages 2 1/2 - 5 years old and Arena kids is for children ages 6-11. 

AM Arena Tots Classes: 9:30am -11:30am 

PM Arena Kids Classes: 6pm - 8pm

There will be NO Arena Kids & Tots on Saturday & Sunday.
NO Arena Kids on Friday.

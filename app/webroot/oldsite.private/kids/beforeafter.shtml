Kids Programs: Before & After School Care

The Arena Club’s Before & After School program is a licensed program for children ages 5-12 years old.  Our goal is to create a fun and safe environment every day after school for children while their parents are hard at work!  The program includes homework time, snack time, organized field games on our year round indoor field, swimming year round, outdoor play on our fields and playground, center time in our classrooms, field trips on early dismissal days and much more!  The Arena Club vans pick up from many local schools please contact us for a complete listing of schools that participate with the program.

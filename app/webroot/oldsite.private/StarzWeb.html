The Arena Club Sports Academy

Welcome to Harford County’s Elite Sports Training Facility. With over 6,000 square feet of turf, three indoor batting cages, floor to ceiling nets and the best sports & conditioning trainers in the area, we are the answer to your young athletes training needs.

Mission: The Arena Club Sports Academy has been created to provide professional instruction to players of all ages.  We aim to promote the game of baseball and softball in the Maryland area and improve the level of play while making a positive influence on our youth.

It’s a Fact: Only 15% of All Little Leaguers Play High School Baseball… Less than 3% of those will play in College.

What Happened to the other 85%?
30% do not move up because of physical inability to play at a higher level. 55% do not move up because of lack of instruction during the developmental years. 


Let STARZ Baseball Academy Staff help improve these numbers for your son!

Aquatics

Whether you swim competitively or recreational you will benefit from our aquatic group exercise classes, therapeutic classes and swim lessons. Our instructors are well-trained, highly motivated and personable. Our lifeguarding staff is professional and courteous. Come experience the wonderful world of water at The Arena Club!

The Aquatics Center features:

  • 25 meter lap/recreational pool.
  • 12-person whirlpool with waterfall.
  • 29 x 34 wading pool with rubberized beach entry and waterfall.
  • 32 x 38 heated saltwater therapy pool.

"Oasis" (our family rec pool) features:

  • "Thunder" and "Lightning" (2 waterpark-sized waterslides)
  • A 9-foot deep diving well.
  • A water basketball court.
  • A 4-foot area for free swim.

The weather policy for all Aquatics Classes
is as follows:

  • On rainy days, aquatic classes will run as scheduled.
  • Once the guards hear thunder and ask class participants to exit the pool, the class is over.
  • The front desk will be notified prior to the start of each class if a weather cancellation is necessary.

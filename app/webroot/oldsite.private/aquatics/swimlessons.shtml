Aquatics: Swim Lessons

Swim School Year Round Program

Our Swim School is structured through the Red Cross to provide a safe and nurturing aquatics experience for the students. From novice to competitive level swimmers, our lessons encompass proper swim instruction, technique and endurance workouts.

FALL weekday swim lessons are Monday and Wednesday from 12:30-2pm during the day and from 4:30-6pm during the evening. Lessons/Sessions are monthly during the school year. Classes are October through May. We also offer private and semi-private lessons which are held on individualized schedules.

All children must be toilet trained in order to take swim lessons, unless enrolled in the infant/parent class. A swim diaper and Tiny Tush plastic cover is required if not toilet trained. Call the Arena Club to enroll in Swim School or pick up a registration form located in the club lobby and submit to our front desk personnel.

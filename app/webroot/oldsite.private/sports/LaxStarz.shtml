Coach Mezzanotte has been involved with lacrosse since 1977 when the Board of Education in Harford County, Maryland approved the inclusion of lacrosse into the interscholastic sports venue. In his 35+ years of coaching experience which included recreation, high school, college and the professional ranks he has always brought his enthusiasm and knowledge at every level. He also co-founded Harford Lacrosse Camp which has been running strong 30+ years for boys and girls ages 5-18. Coach Mezzanotte is also proud to boast of his teaching career which spans 35 plus years. He is a certified K-12 teacher and has taught at every level with elementary being his favorite age level.

Coach Mezzanotte's Philosophy
“Every kid counts." He likes to work with each kid individually to improve their weaknesses and build confidence through their strengths.

The Lax Starz Academy is a great program for players of all abilities. Coach Mezzanotte focuses on repetition of fundamentals, which are needed to have success at higher levels of lacrosse. Most college coaches are not impressed with the “razzle, dazzle stuff” but want kids to come into a college setting with the proper fundamentals to succeed. The Lax Starz Academy has an onsite sport performance trainer during strength and conditioning. We use state-of-the-art technology through sports cords, dynamic warm-ups, reaction drills, and more to prepare your player for their upcoming season.

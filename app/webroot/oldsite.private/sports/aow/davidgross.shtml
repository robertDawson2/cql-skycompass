Sports : Athlete of the Week

David Gross

The Arena Club would like to Congratulate ACA Swim Team Member David Gross as this weeks Athlete of the Week!

This past Thursday-Sunday the swim team took 26 swimmers to the Maryland State Championships.

14 swimmers had 38 Swims finish in Top-10
6 swimmers had 13 Swims place Top-3
3 Swimmers won 5 events

David Gross won the Men's Open 1000 Freestyle (among several other members of the ACA Swim Team who were champions.) Prior to this year The Arena Club did not have a male champion.

David Gross was awarded the 2011-2012 Maryland Swimmer of the Year for 15 year old boys.

Congratulations David!

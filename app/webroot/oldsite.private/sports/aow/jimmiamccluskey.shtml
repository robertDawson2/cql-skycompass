Sports : Athlete of the Week

Jimmia McCluskey

The Arena Club would like to Congratulate Aberdeen High School's Jimmia McCluskey as this weeks Athlete of the Week

At the UCBAC Track and Field Meet, Jimmia came in First Place in the following events:

100 yd. (12.15 seconds)
200 yd. (25.16 seconds)
Long Jump (18 Feet, 9.75 Inches)

She also anchored her 4x100 relay team who came in First Place, as well. Jimmia will be attending Temple University in the Fall on a Track and Field Athletic Scholarship.

Congratulations Jimmia!

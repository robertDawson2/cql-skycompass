Sports : Athlete of the Week

Allen Bryant

.

The Arena Club would like to Congratulate John Carroll Sophmore Allen Bryant as this weeks Athlete of the Week

Allen Rushed 7 times for 124 yards including a 64 yard Touchdown run on the first play from scrimmage. Also caught 2 passes for 51 yards. Helped John Carroll defeat 6time state champion Delmar (DE) 28-7 in Delaware. Allen is averaging over 12 yards a carry in 3 games.

Allen started playing varsity last year as a freshmen. He is also a member of the track and lacrosse teams at John Carroll. He has done community service helping under privledged schools get school supplies. He is a hard worker both on and off the field. Has a passion for football. Played in the All American game as an 8th grader in Cowboy stadium.

Congratulations Allen!

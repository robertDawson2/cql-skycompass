Sports : Athlete of the Week

Congratulations Maggie Gallagher - 2014 Athlete of the Year!

The Arena Club’s “Athlete of the Week” is a scholarship program that recognizes outstanding local high school athletes. Each week The Arena Club will select a high school athlete based on nominations from area coaches. Weekly winners will receive a performance “Athlete of the Week” t-shirt and will be featured on The Arena Club’s website, Facebook page, road marquee, and print publications. At the end of the school year, a $1,000 scholarship will be awarded to one of the weekly winners to go towards the college of their choice. The high school of the final winning athlete will also receive six free Arena Speed and Power training sessions for that athlete’s sports team for the following season.

Weekly winners will be chosen from students from 9th-12th grade, however the final $1,000 scholarship will be awarded to a senior. Coaches can nominate athletes by filling out the nomination form, or emailing Coach Mike Edge at coachedge@thearenaclub.com

We are very excited to be able to honor and recognize our local high school athletes and sports teams, and we hope parents, coaches and students are equally excited and take advantage of this great opportunity.

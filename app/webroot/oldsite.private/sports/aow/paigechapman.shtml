Sports : Athlete of the Week

Paige Chapman

 

The Arena Club would like to Congratulate Harford Tech Track & Field athlete, Paige Chapman, as this week's Athlete of the Week!

This past Saturday at the State Track & Field Championships Paige won the girls 2A 100-meter dash, and placed 5th in the 200-meter dash, and Long Jump events. She was the only Harford County track athlete to win a state title at this year's meet.

Congratulations Paige!

Sports : Athlete of the Week

Justin Smith

The Arena Club would like to Congratulate Harford Tech pitcher, Justin Smith, as this week's Athlete of the Week!

Justin, a junior at Harford Tech, earned victory this week by pitching 5 strong innings to defeat the North Harford Hawks, giving them their first loss of the season. Justin only allowed five hits and one earned run, while striking out five North Harford batters. Justin was also 3-for-4 earning a double, two runs, and one RBI. As well as being an outsdanding player, Justin maintins a GPA above 3.0.

 

Congratulations Justin!

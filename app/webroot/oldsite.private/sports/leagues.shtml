Sports: Adult Leagues

Our indoor field is unlike any other in the area!

Grains of rubber subsurface and synthetic field grass ease the impact on your pre and post season play. Our field simulates a perfectly natural grass surface. Our premier rubber infill surface allows players to increase speed and performance and enhance their stability, speed, and traction. The sidelines are not bound by walls, but instead the freedom of netting creates a more realistic barrier which allows teams to maximize their outdoor game.

Field Rentals are also available for practices, evaluations, group rentals, and parties. Contact Tim Deyesu for more information.

Adult Indoor Soccer Leagues

Unlimited Coed, Men and Women 18+
Day: Tuesday & Wednesday
Game Times:  8, 9 and 10 p.m. (11 p.m. games, based on number of teams registered)
League Fees: $489 per eight game session
Ref fees: $176 ($22 per game per team plus league fee for a team total of $665)

*Team deposit of $200 is required to register. The team balance is due prior to the start of the first game. If the total fee is not paid in full before the first game, a team representative must submit a Visa or Mastercard number to keep on file. If the full balance is not submitted by game two, the balance due will be processed on the credit card. All first place teams will receive $200 off of their team fee for the FOLLOWING SESSION ONLY

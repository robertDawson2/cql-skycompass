There’s a new excitement building up at The Arena Club...and the year is only just beginning. Our passion for sports training has elevated to a new level with our new 360 Sports Performance Institute (S.P.I.).

360SPI was developed with the concept of offering athletes a complete 360 degree package of services so they can develop to their full potential—all under one roof. From the beginner player all the way to college-level athlete, the 360 Sports Performance Institute offers athletes cutting edge sports performance training to meet the high demand needs for any sport. Ackerman leads a team of highly educated sports trainers who carry degrees as well as specialized sports training certifications from NSPA, NASM, Nike SPARQ, MORR, CSPS and more. Qadry Ismail’s Missile Training adds another level of experience and services to the team. “Qadry’s multi-sport achievements and experience at the professional level bring an entirely new dimension to our training services,” said Ackerman.

The Arena Club’s 360 Sports Performance Institute currently offers the following programs:

Missile Training w/Qadry Ismail
Missile Training is built on the premise that “Form Dictates Function,” therefore proper form dictates a more consistent performance. Through specific protocols, Missile Training targets weaknesses in the athletes movements that can restrict proper athletic performance. The design of Missile Training is to limit the compensations of the muscular skeletal system so that an athlete’s body is able to endure higher levels of training and excel in real time game play. Missile Training protocols will teach the athlete’s nervous system how to properly power on and power off at will, which will increase the harmony of muscle movement within their body. Missile Training added to any traditional sports training program is guaranteed to make an athlete stronger, more powerful, have increased velocity, an explosive first step quickness, greater lateral agility, and maximize balance and coordination.

M.O.R.R. Movement Training
Movement Overspeed Resistance Reaction is a training system that provides our athletes a revolutionary approach to developing movement. We are able to provide our athletes the opportunity to train in 360 degrees of movement increasing their speed, agility, quickness and creating complete athleticism.

Sports Specific Training
Whether its learning sport-specific lifts in the weight room, developing proper fundamentals in a sport on the turf field or having a nutritional consultation to discuss proper performance nutrition for student athletes, the S.P.I. team can provide it all at one location. This training is focused and designed to meet an athlete’s specific needs in his or her sport. Our expert staff designs programs and drills that are sport and position specific for athletes of all levels and ages. A nationally recognized sports nutritionist can design athlete specific meal plans, upon request.

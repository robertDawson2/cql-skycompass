Sports: Youth Clinics

The Arena Club offers numerous youth sport clinics.

Baseball:

Starz Baseball Clinics
Starz Baseball clinics are designed for players of all abilities and will focus on developing strong fundamentals as they lead into their season. Click here for the flyer and registration form!

Soccer:

U4/U6/U8 Coed Soccer Clinics
Our soccer programs introduce the fundamentals of the game. Allow your child to learn, have fun, and meet new friends! For more information, click here.

Daytime Youth Soccer Clinics (2.5-6 years old)
Our soccer programs introduce the fundamentals of the game. Allow your child to learn, have fun, and meet new friends! For more information, click here.

Lacrosse:

Lax Academy
Join us for a program designed for athletes who are committed to improving their game. This is an intense program that is focused on challenging each athlete in all aspects of Lacrosse. For more information, click here.

Girls Lax Clinic
Girls ages K-5th grade will be divided in their age group to work with a coach on various skills and drills to improve fundamentals of the game. Differentiated instruction will be provided for beginners and players looking for more advanced skill refinement For more information, click here.

Flag Football:

Youth Flag Football
Our program is associated with NFL Flag Football and is designed to educate young people about football while emphasizing participation and sportsmanship. Players learn skills and lessons that help them succeed both on and off the field. Information for next session here.

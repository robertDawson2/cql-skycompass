Arena Wrestling Academy

The Arena Wrestling Academy is for boys and girls that are looking to gain confidence, discipline and determination from a sport. We will accept all wrestlers, from beginners to advanced, and have a set training schedule in place so that all levels will excel. AWA teaches participants the necessary life skills that will benefit them throughout their lives. It is important that every wrestler leaves the academy feeling a sense of accomplishment and self-worth.

The Arena Wrestling Academy (AWA) is Harford County's Premiere Training Program for Wrestling. This academy boasts all-star staff including, Gary Siler (Director of Wrestling, National Wrestling Hall of Fame MD Chapter inductee), Jay LaValley (Director of Kolat Wrestling), Danny Whitenak (accomplished Kolat Wrestling Coach), and guest clinicians throughout the year.

AWA also has a top notch facility to help your wrestler succeed on the mat. Clinicians will help wrestlers develop techniques and skills to succeed in all wrestling disciplines. This is the only program in the area to offer a year round training academy dedicated to wrestling.
